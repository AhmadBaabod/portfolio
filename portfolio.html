// about me

Who is Ahmad?

Hey! I'm Ahmad Baabod — a CS student at Effat University in Jeddah, concentrating in Artificial Intelligence. My journey didn't start in a classroom though.

It was 2020. Corona had the world on pause, and a bored high schooler stuck at home decided to crack open the internet and ask: how does any of this actually work? That curiosity never left. What started as poking around tutorials became an obsession with understanding systems, writing code, and building things from scratch.

I pick things up fast — not because I rush, but because I genuinely want to understand. That drive has pulled me across JavaScript, C++, C#, backend APIs, and now deep into AI. My GitHub is less a portfolio and more a map of everywhere my curiosity has taken me.

Currently sharpening my focus on AI & software development, and always looking for the next interesting problem to dig into.
